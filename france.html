Eiffel Tower

The "Iron Lady" was initially a temporary structure for the 1889 World's Fair but quickly became a permanent global icon of France. The tower offers visitors three distinct levels, including fine-dining restaurants and a top-level observation deck. From its summit, tourists are treated to breathtaking, panoramic views over the romantic City of Lights.
