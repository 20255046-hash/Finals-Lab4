Colosseum

Built by the Flavian emperors and completed in 80 CE, the Colosseum stands as a testament to Roman engineering. It offered ancient citizens spectacular entertainment, including gladiatorial battles, dramatic re-enactments, and animal hunts. Today, visitors can explore the massive arena floor and the intricate underground hypogeum system, connecting directly with the history and brutality of Imperial Rome.
